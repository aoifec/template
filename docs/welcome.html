Welcome to Swrve!

It’s easy to get started — just follow the 3-step onboarding guide below! Want to see what you can do with Swrve? Check out the Swrve videos. If you want to start experimenting with Swrve right away, then jump right in with the Swrve app.

1. Add your App

Add your app’s platform titles to the Swrve service and nominate the lead developer responsible for integrating each title. Swrve then emails your developer to prepare them to start the integration process.

2. Integrate Your App

Integrating the core functionality of Swrve into your app is quick and easy. Your nominated lead developer integrates the SDK with just one line of code to enable you to access basic analytics and Swrve’s in-app messaging functionality. After that, there’s just a few additional steps to complete the core integration — then it’s up to you just how sophisticated you get using Swrve’s advanced integration options.

3. Engage Your Users

Start using Swrve to track what your app users are doing, learn from their behavior, build personalized relationships with them, and engage with them in a relevant, meaningful way.

Ready to Start?

If you’re new to Swrve, take a quick tour of the Swrve platform and then get started by adding your app’s platform titles. For more information, see Add Your App. If you have questions before you start, contact Swrve Technical Support at support@swrve.com.

In the meantime, if you want to try out Swrve right away, use the Swrve app.

The Swrve App

The Swrve app gives you a taste of the features that Swrve has to offer — you can start experimenting with segments, A/B testing, in-app messages and push notifications — all without having to integrate your app. For more information, see Using the Swrve App.
